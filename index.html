What is Linda-lea's Supports?

Linda-lea's Supports is an empathetic group that tailors to the individuals needs through sensory and behavioural management.

We work with ALL ages, including NDIS plan managed and self managed participants.

We help enable people with disabilities to exercise choice and control in the pursuit of their goals, planning and of course, delivery of their supports.

We have completed courses in early childcare special needs in voice control and muscle manipulation therapy, as well as courses in "Triple P" (Positive Parenting Program), "They're driving me Nuts" (Toddlers Program Support) and St John of God (Child Awareness Program).

We believe in boosting self-esteem and positive reinforcement methods, including music to soothe the soul.

We are friendly, open-minded and easy going, ready to help those in need in all aspects of support; from taking you to appointments, to helping around the house or just having some respite and a chin wag.

We are here to help you.
